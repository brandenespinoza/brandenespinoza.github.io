## Elementary School Years
* **[There's No Such Thing as a Dragon](https://www.amazon.com/Theres-No-Such-Thing-Dragon/dp/0375851372)** by Jack Kent - Big problems are small if you don't ignore them. 
* **[Oh, The Places You Will Go](https://www.amazon.com/Oh-Places-Youll-Go-Dr-Seuss-audiobook/dp/B000WBZ7EQ/)** by Dr. Seuss - Pursue you dreams, overcome obstacles. 
* **[Peter Pan](https://www.amazon.com/Peter-Pan-Illustrated-Original-Illustrations/dp/9916987041)** by J.M. Barrie - Growing up is a necessary part of life.
* **[The Giving Tree](https://www.amazon.com/Giving-Tree-Shel-Silverstein/dp/0060256656/)** by Shel Silverstein - Through sacrifice we find meaning. 
* **[The Tuttle Twins (12 Book Series)](https://tuttletwins.com/product/tuttle-twins-combo-pack-plus-free-bonuses/)** by Connor Boyack - Life. Liberty. Property.
* **[Hatchet](https://www.amazon.com/Hatchet-Gary-Paulsen/dp/1416936475)** by Gary Paulsen - That manly desire to carve out life from wilderness and chaos.
* **[My Side of the Mountain](https://www.amazon.com/Side-Mountain-Jean-Craighead-George/dp/0525463461/)** by Jean Craighead George - Do dangerous things, carefully.
* **[The Adventures of Tom Sawyer](https://www.amazon.com/Adventures-Tom-Sawyer-Original-Illustrations/dp/1955529191/)** by Mark Twain - Go. Do. 
* **[The Chronicles of Narnia](https://www.amazon.com/Chronicles-Narnia-C-S-Lewis/dp/0060598247/)** by C.S. Lewis - Redemption and salvation. 


## Middle School Years
* **[The Hobbit](https://www.amazon.com/Hobbit-Illustrated-J-R-R-Tolkien/dp/0544174224)** by J.R.R. Tolkien - Courage. Loyalty. Determination. Wisdom. Beware the dangers of greed and selfishness.
* **[Lord of the Flies](https://www.amazon.com/Lord-Flies-William-Golding/dp/0399501487)** by William Golding - Natural man is a savage. Power and fear corrupt absolutely. 
* **[Brave New World](https://www.amazon.com/Brave-New-World-Aldous-Huxley/dp/0060850523/)** by Aldous Huxley - Be aware of the power of technology to control and manipulate human behavior. Don't sacrifice liberty and autonomy.

## High School Years
* [**The Book of Mormon**](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/17) - The roots of the family culture you inherited. It will make your mom happy.
* [**Atlas Shrugged**](https://www.amazon.com/Atlas-Shrugged-Centennial-Ed-Rand/dp/0525948929/) - Glorification of the best within us. Pride in producing.
* [**The Odyssey**](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/1727) - Leave home. Pursue glory. Come back. Redeem the fallen.

## College Years
* [**Human Action**](https://www.amazon.com/Human-Action-Scholars-Ludwig-Mises) - The most significant book on the economics of liberty you will ever own. 
* [**The Book of Mormon**](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/17) - Again. This time with purpose. Find out if it is true.

## Family Building Years
* [**Parent Effectiveness Training**](https://www.amazon.com/Parent-Effectiveness-Training-T-Responsible/dp/B001RMWAXK/) - You're a rookie. We all are. 
* [**Dumbing Us Down: the Hidden Curriculum of Compulsory Schooling**](https://www.amazon.com/Dumbing-Down-Curriculum-Compulsory-Anniversary/dp/0865714487) - The leviathan stole so much tradition from us. 
* [**The Book of Mormon**](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/17) - Again. Because everything changes with a family.
